#### Story of the day

In the Amstel Gold Race, a breakaway of seven riders went clear early on, but the peloton reeled them back in with 36 km remaining.

On the Keutenberg a powerful group of eleven riders broke away. From that move, Michał Kwiatkowski launched a decisive attack, with Benoît Cosnefroy bridging across.

In the sprint to the line, Cosnefroy thought he had taken the victory, but the photo finish revealed that Kwiatkowski had edged him out, securing his second win in the Amstel Gold Race.
