#### Story of the day

A nine-rider break formed early. Van Baarle and Fred Wright gained a small lead with 45 km remaining. Pogacar accelerated on the Koppenberg, joined by Madouas and Van der Poel, and the trio caught the two leaders. On the final Oude Kwaremont, Pogacar attacked again, and only Van der Poel could follow. In the last few hundred meters, Madouas and Van Baarle returned, but Van der Poel triumphed in the sprint.
