#### Story of the day

In Stage 5 of the Skoda Tour de Luxembourg, a 12-rider breakaway got away while UAE and Groupama controlled the peloton. With 25 km to go, Healy and Gregoire attacked from the peloton and caught the final escapee, Wenzel, who later dropped back. Gregoire made his decisive move 1.1 km from the finish to win the final stage. McNulty secured the GC victory. This marked UAE’s 86th win of the season, setting a new record.
