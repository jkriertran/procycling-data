#### Story of the day

The Gooikse Pijl was held in rainy conditions, where the big favorite Jonathan Milan had to abandon early. After 62 kilometers, four riders broke away. Jayco and Picnick Post NL set the pace in the peloton. Axel Källberg crashed out of the lead group. The last attackers were caught 3.6 kilometers from the line. In the final sprint, Oded Kogut crossed the line first.
