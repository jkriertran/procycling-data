#### Story of the day

An early break of eight riders formed. Just after the cobbles of the Trouée d’Arenberg, Van der Poel attacked with Pogacar responding, leaving a group of five favourites. Pedersen, Philipsen, and Bissegger one by one lost contact with the front group. With 38.1 km remaining, Pogacar missed a corner and crashed. Van der Poel rode solo to victory despite a puncture, claiming his third consecutive Paris-Roubaix.
