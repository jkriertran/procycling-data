#### Story of the day

After several early attacks, eleven riders managed to break away. The breakaway was eventually caught after the Cipressa. On the Poggio, Tadej Pogačar had his teammates pulling before attacking, with Mathieu van der Poel joining him. A chasing group of ten riders managed to bridge back to the front. Mohorič, Pidcock and Sobrero then launched late attacks. Van der Poel worked hard to bring them back, allowing his teammate Jasper Philipsen to win the sprint.
