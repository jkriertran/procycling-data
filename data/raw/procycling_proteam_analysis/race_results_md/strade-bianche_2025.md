#### Story of the day

A six-man early break formed before UAE Team Emirates – XRG raised the pace on the Monte Sante Marie. Connor Swift attacked from the break to go solo. With 78.5 km remaining, Pidcock and Pogacar accelerated to join him. After Pogacar crashed, Pidcock did not go full gas, allowing the Slovenian to rejoin a few kilometers later. With 18.6 km to go, Pogacar attacked again to take victory. With his win, he equalled record holder Fabian Cancellara
