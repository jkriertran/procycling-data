#### Story of the day

Eight riders formed an early break in cold, wet conditions, with Silvan Dillier controlling the move until 50 km from the finish. The weather cleared as the race reached the coast. The last escapee was caught 26 km from the line. On the Cipressa, Pogacar attacked, joined by Van der Poel and Ganna. Ganna lost contact after Pogacar’s acceleration on the Poggio but rejoined. Van der Poel launched the sprint early and claimed victory.
