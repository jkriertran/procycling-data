#### Story of the day

Nine riders formed the early breakaway but never gained more than three minutes on the peloton. The break was caught before the Cipressa. On the Poggio, Tim Wellens led out Tadej Pogačar before his attack, with Filippo Ganna, Mathieu van der Poel and Wout van Aert following. Just before the summit Van der Poel attacked, entered the descent with a small gap and held on to win.
