#### Story of the day

Early in the race, Ineos caused a split in the peloton, with many favorites in the second group. With 110 km to go, five riders formed a lead group including Mohoric, they were chased by agroup of twelve favorites. At 28 km, the last escapee was caught by a new attack of three riders. With 19.5 km remaining, Van Baarle attacked, extended his lead, and won solo.
