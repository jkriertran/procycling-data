#### Story of the day

Quinn Simmons was the first attacker of the day and part of a strong 14-rider break. UAE Team Emirates – XRG and Red Bull – BORA – hansgrohe controlled the break from the peloton. With 83 km remaining, Simmons went solo. On the Passo di Ganda, UAE set a fierce pace before Pogacar attacked, catching Simmons, who briefly held his wheel before being dropped. Pogacar rode the final 33.7 km alone to claim his fifth straight Il Lombardia, equalling Fausto Coppi’s record.
