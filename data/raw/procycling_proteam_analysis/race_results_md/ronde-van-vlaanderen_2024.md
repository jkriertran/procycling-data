#### Story of the day

An early break of eight riders shaped the race. With 100 km to go, Mads Pedersen accelerated, splitting the peloton into several groups. He attacked again and got one rider with him, but the move didn’t last. On the slippery Koppenberg, where many riders had to walk, Van der Poel went solo with 44.8 km remaining, winning his third Tour of Flanders and equaling the record.
